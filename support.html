Frequently Asked Questions

How much does iMix Premium cost?

iMix Premium is $9.99/month with a free 7-day trial. You get full access to all features during the trial. Cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

How do I start the free trial?

When you open the app, tap "Start Free Trial" on the subscription screen. You'll get 7 days of full access. Your Apple ID will be charged $9.99 after the trial unless you cancel.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings on your iPhone > tap your name at the top > Subscriptions > iMix Premium > Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

I subscribed but features are still locked. What do I do?

Try the "Restore Purchases" option in the app's paywall screen. If the issue persists, make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the subscription, then contact us.

How do I use effects?

While a song is playing, tap the FX button to trigger tempo-synced audio effects. Tap the vinyl record icon to play DJ scratch sounds. Long-press and hold the vinyl icon to auto-alternate between scratch styles.

Can I import my own songs?

Yes! iMix Premium subscribers can import their own audio stems from the Files app. Load up to 7 custom tracks and mix them with the full effects engine.

Does iMix Music require an internet connection?

No. All audio is bundled in the app. The only internet connection used is for processing subscription purchases through the App Store.

What devices are supported?

iMix Music requires iOS 17.0 or later and runs on iPhone and iPad.
