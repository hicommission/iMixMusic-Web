Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock full songs?

Tap the "Unlock" button when prompted after the 40-second preview. This is a one-time purchase of $1.99 that unlocks full playback for all songs — no subscription required.

I purchased but songs are still locked. What do I do?

Try the "Restore Purchases" option in the app. If the issue persists, make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase, then contact us.

How do I use effects?

While a song is playing, tap the FX button to trigger tempo-synced audio effects. Tap the vinyl record icon to play DJ scratch sounds. Long-press and hold the vinyl icon to auto-alternate between scratch styles.

Can I import my own songs?

Custom stem import is coming in a future update. Currently, iMix Music includes curated multi-track songs optimized for mixing.

Does iMix Music require an internet connection?

No. All audio is bundled in the app. The only internet connection used is for processing In-App Purchases through the App Store.

What devices are supported?

iMix Music requires iOS 17.0 or later and runs on iPhone and iPad.
