iMix Music

Privacy Policy

No data collected

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

HiCommission Music ("we", "us", "our") built iMix Music as a commercial application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. iMix Music operates entirely offline on your device.

  • No account required — you never need to sign up or log in.
  • No analytics or tracking — we do not use any analytics SDKs.
  • No advertising — the app contains no ads or ad networks.
  • No network requests — the app does not connect to the internet except for subscription transactions processed by Apple.

Subscriptions & In-App Purchases

iMix Music offers a monthly auto-renewable subscription ("iMix Premium") at $9.99/month with a 7-day free trial. All subscription and payment transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment information such as credit card numbers or billing details.

Subscription management (cancellation, renewal, etc.) is handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Audio Data

All audio stems and samples are bundled within the app. Any audio you interact with stays on your device. We do not upload, stream, or transmit any audio data.

Children's Privacy

iMix Music does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since the app collects no data from any user, it is safe for all ages.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Apple's StoreKit framework for processing subscriptions. Apple's privacy policy governs those transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
